Apply to be a magistrate

Use this form to apply to be a magistrate in England and Wales.

You do not need legal training or formal qualifications to become a magistrate.

You must be able to:

You can buy:

  • commit to up 13 days a year to sit as a magistrate
  • be over 18 years old
  • demonstrate you have the key qualities to be a magistrate
  • not be seeking asylum or leave to remain

You will be expected to live or work near the area you want to serve and be able to show a good understanding of local social issues.
