Who’s it for

  • For all agencies across the Ministry of Justice

Benefits

  • Get a service online quicker and cheaper
  • Easy to maintain
  • Avoid the cost of investing in tools to build your own service
  • Uses recognised GOV.UK design patterns
  • Get quicker feedback from stakeholders (policy teams and frontline staff)

Support

  • Work with you to help design a better service
  • Conduct usability testing of forms with users
  • We’ll publish and host the service for you
  • Technical support offered through MOJ
  • Provide analytics so you can see whether users are finding your service difficult

Why make services digital?

Digital services are better for users. Online, you can:

  • Reduce the number of user errors
  • Reduce paper processing
  • Reduce time spent on manual data entry
  • Provides an easy way for policy teams to gather feedback from frontline staff
  • Reduce the marginal cost of transactions for legal professionals and administration
  • Provide detailed quantitative analysis for which parts of the forms users find difficult
  • Reduce the amount of unnecessary contact time where users call, email or come in person to ask “where’s my thing?”. (With the use of GOV.UK Notify
  • No lengthy contract
  • Understand your users better and respond to their feedback
  • Simplify complicated guidance and user journeys
  • Offer assistance to users with accessibility needs

You’ll need

  • To follow the GDS Service Standards
  • You service should have less than 100,000 transactions a year. If it has more than 100,000 transactions, contact the MOJ Digital team.
