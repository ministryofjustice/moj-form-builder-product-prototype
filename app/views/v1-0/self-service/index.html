About the Government Digital Strategy

The Government Digital Strategy sets out how the government will become digital by default.

  • services need to meet the needs of their users across all channels
  • become an organisation that is digital by default
  • ensure that government data is properly managed, protected and (where non-sensitive) made available and shared effectively
  • creating shared platforms, components and reusable business capabilities: continuing with government as a platform, reducing duplication, cost and increasing efficiency across government
